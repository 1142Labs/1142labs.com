Harm Reduction · Priority Resources

Safety First

These are the most important resources. Before anything else — know your substances, test your substances, and have a safety plan.

💬
TripSit

Real-time harm reduction chat with trained volunteers. Drug combo checker. The most accessible first-response resource for any substance emergency.

tripsit.me →
📖
Psychonaut Wiki

The most comprehensive independently-maintained database of psychoactive substances. Subjective effect index, dosage guides, pharmacology, and harm reduction for every compound.

psychonautwiki.org →
🌿
Erowid

The original. Decades of user experience reports, chemistry data, and harm reduction documentation. Essential historical archive of psychoactive substance knowledge.

erowid.org →
🧪
DanceSafe

Drug checking services and testing kits. Fentanyl test strips, MDMA reagent kits, and educational materials. Physical harm reduction at events and online orders.

dancesafe.org →
🔬
The Bunk Police

Reagent test kits and drug checking supplies. Marquis, Mecke, Mandelin, Simon's, fentanyl strips. Testing saves lives — this is where you get the kits.

bunkpolice.com →
