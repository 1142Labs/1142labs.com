Research Log · Breakthrough Timeline

The Log

Key observations in chronological order. The living record of the 1142 research program.

December 22, 2019 · 11:42 PM
Entry 001 — The First 11:42
Solo 12-hour LSD session beginning exactly at 11:42 PM, concluding at 11:42 AM. Basement bachelor apartment, 18 years old, first lease. Something fundamentally shifts. The idea cannot be contained. 1142 Labs is born — not as a brand, as a rejection of every broken thing the world said to accept. Duration: 12 hours. Compound: LSD-25. Setting: Peterborough.
March 2020
Entry 002 — Brainstorm Nights Protocol
First structured research protocol established. LSD-25 + low-dose methylphenidate (Ritalin) in combination. Objective: creative exploration and real-time data capture. Finding: the stimulant prevents "scatter" during the psychedelic state — the user functions as an operator within their own mind. Creative output measurably enhanced vs. solo LSD sessions. First use of combined session documentation methodology.
July 2020
Entry 003 — 11-42.ca Goes Live
First digital manifestation of 1142. Site v1.0 launched as a repository for experience reports and psychedelic data. The movement has a URL. CFNY 102.1 The Edge (Toronto) references the project: "2 southern Ontario scientists are tripping out and testing the effects of acid on autism." First mainstream media acknowledgment. The underground goes above ground, briefly.
October 2020
Entry 004 — Medication Recalibration
High-dose methylphenidate regimen commenced. Key realization: neurodivergent cognitive needs differ fundamentally from the populations mainstream dosing guidelines were built for. The brain is an operating system. The medication is a software update. MPH Series paper development begins.
June 2021
Entry 005 — Oxidized Methylphenidate Theory (MPH-005)
Theory formalized: methylphenidate in an oxidized state may possess unique cognitive-enhancing properties when used during a psychedelic session. Specific: the chemical state of the molecule changes its interaction with neurotransmitter systems, producing a qualitatively distinct quality of clarity. "Visionary speed." Documented as MPH-005. One of the most technically speculative entries in the archive.
February 2022
Entry 006 — LSD Memory Archive Discovery (CS-001)
Observation: LSD functions as a tool for unlocking repressed traumatic memories and navigating deeply buried subconscious material. The brain stores trauma like digital files — LSD provides the access key. Sessions designed specifically around this model show consistent pattern: suppressed material becomes retrievable, emotionally processable, and integrable within 48 hours post-session. CS-001 formally written.
April 2023
Entry 007 — Global Ritual Expansion
The 11:42 ritual — drop at sunset on the 22nd, maintain for 12 hours — expands beyond solo practice to a globally synchronized movement. Participants in multiple locations, same temporal window. Hypothesis: collective synchronized psychedelic exploration yields higher-order insights than isolated sessions. The 11:42 becomes a form of distributed consciousness computing.
November 2023
Entry 008 — Prescription Reinstatement, Barrie
Successful public advocacy effort in Barrie, Ontario results in official medical recognition and reinstatement of prescription support. Validation of the 1142 approach to neurodivergent medication. A turning point — from underground to partially recognized. The movement enters a phase of stabilization.
January 2024
Entry 009 — Studied Substances Published
Comprehensive archive published documenting every psychoactive substance analyzed by 1142 Labs over five years of independent research. The chem log goes public. Every compound, every protocol, every harm reduction finding — accessible. The underground library opens its doors.
