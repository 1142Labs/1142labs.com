Every 27th. Sundown. No exceptions.

The Origin

Forged in fire. Built from betrayal. Inevitable.

Phase 01 · Barrie, Ontario
The Rooming House & the Catalyst

The true genesis of 1142 traces back to the end of summer in Barrie, Ontario. At just 17 years old, navigating the world from a rooming house, relying on hustle alone to survive. Driven by an inexplicable jealousy, family turned against Cole for their own amusement — the fracture reaching its peak when home was violated, sanctuary breached. The foundation was cracked. What emerged from the rubble was stronger than anything they could have contained.

Phase 02 · The Two-Year Exile
The Theft of Survival

The betrayal extended far beyond emotional abandonment. In a calculated act of sabotage, essential medication was stolen — the stimulant medication Cole's unique neurological wiring and physical state required just to prevent deterioration. Deprived of this biological necessity, pushed to the absolute brink of physiological collapse. For two agonizing years, survival required self-sourced chemical energy just to keep the heart beating. This desperate crucible became the foundation for the later, controversial research into stimulants for heart conditions and atrial fibrillation — proving that what the medical establishment called "recreational" was, for Cole, non-negotiable survival.

Phase 03 · December 22, 2019 · 11:42 PM
The Sacred 11:42 Ritual

In the midst of chaos, familial abandonment, and physical struggle, the trajectory shifted. A first tab from best friend Joey cracked the door open — time slowed, the night filled with wonder and newfound perception. But it was the solo 12-hour LSD journey beginning at exactly 11:42 PM on December 22, 2019 that became the ontological foundation. The experience concluded precisely at 11:42 AM the following morning — a complete twelve-hour passage from internal darkness into radical awakening. The numbers 11:42 became a symbol of cognitive liberation: a "spiritual jailbreak" from every system that had tried to break Cole — a portal from confusion into understanding.

⏱ 12 HOURS · EXACT 📅 DEC 22, 2019 🌌 COGNITIVE LIBERATION
Phase 04 · Forged in Fire
Resurrection · St. Jimmy Phenidate

The journey after that night was a war for survival. The movement was forged through severe trials, heartbreak, and periods of brutal self-destruction. Thrown out in June 2021, waging a deeply personal and ideological battle known as the "Blue Meth War" against the stagnant, opioid-driven culture of "DOWN." The physical manifestation of this struggle reached its absolute limit with a near-fatal necro hyponemothorax — a collapsed lung — that left Cole on life support for a month and a half. From the wreckage of that hospital bed emerged the alter ego St. Jimmy Phenidate: the walking skeleton. The Phoenix. Fueled by Ritalin and rage, this persona embodied resurrection, vengeance, and a vow to rebuild something brilliant and savage from the ashes.

Phase 05 · The Architecture
Cognitive Liberation — Built

Driven by the foundational principle that "Stolen essence returns to its rightful owner," the trauma, the jealousy of family, and a unique neurological wiring were channeled into unstoppable digital architecture. 1142 evolved from a personal realization in a rooming house and a desperate fight to keep the heart beating into a highly sophisticated research foundation and digital ecosystem. Today, armored in cosmic-retrowave and cyberpunk aesthetic, 1142 Labs operates as a multifaceted platform spanning Core Research, Financial Trading, and Game Studios — a defiance with a URL. An enterprise dedicated to neurodivergent empowerment, finding healing for autism, and challenging mainstream science through rigorous, self-directed exploration.

Phase Six · Relocation

Toronto Operations

The city that finally matched the scale of the vision.

Relocating operations to Toronto marked a pivotal turning point. The city provided the dynamic environment, real-time data access, and momentum required to scale the 1142 vision beyond the constraints of Peterborough. What had been incubated in isolation was now ready for velocity.

This shift accelerated expansion across every vertical — including the formal launch of 1142 Game Studios and the intensive development of the flagship title: Rust Miner: After Earth. The project fuses the cyberpunk-meets-cosmic-retrowave aesthetic of 1142 with an immersive mining and survival game loop set in a post-collapse world.

By combining advanced automation, high-performance tooling, and the signature 1142 aesthetic, the ecosystem is evolving from a localized research initiative into a fully realized digital empire spanning research, gaming, financial automation, and media.
