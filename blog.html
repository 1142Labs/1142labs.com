Platform Genealogy

Site Versions

Six iterations of the same unstoppable idea.

1.0
11-42.ca · The First Broadcast
The original platform. Raw psychedelic field notes published direct. First public sharing of personal experiences and chemical observations. The seed that cracked the concrete.
JUL 2020 · LAUNCH
2.0
1142 OFFICIAL SITE 2.0 · Wix Era Begins
Migrated to Wix. First structured CMS. 27 blog posts published across substance research, chronicles, and origin lore. Sections for LSD-25, Psilocybin, Mescaline, DMT, PCP. The research vocabulary begins to formalize.
DEC 2022 · CREATED · coleeverdark.wixsite.com/website-1
3.0
1142 v3.0 · The Expansion
Peak blog era. 49+ posts across every category — origins, substances, St. Jimmy lore, field reports, Katrina tributes, persona dispatches. The Persona Matrix fully documented. The legend of Saint Jimmy formalized in writing for the first time.
2023 · FULL ARCHIVE BUILD
4.0
Creator of 1147 · Specialized Fork
Experimental branch. Research deepens into methylphenidate series and Down Syndrome neuroprotection. The 1147 designation represents a specific chemical variant research thread — a side-channel exploration that fed back into the main 1142 canon.
2023–2024 · RESEARCH FORK
5.0
The Edge of Understanding / Breakthroughs Today
Dual-track v5: "Edge of Understanding" and "Future Breakthroughs Today." Full CMS deployment — categories (Journal Entries, How To), projects gallery, PDF library schema, product/store infrastructure. The ecosystem broadens from blog to platform.
2025 · PLATFORM EXPANSION
6.0
1142labs.com · The Fortress
Full custom build. No CMS dependency. Pure HTML/CSS/JS with FX1142 visual system. 13-page ecosystem spanning research, gaming, about, vision, archive, chemicals, chemlog, blog, calculators, resources, toke times, posters, network. Toronto operations. This is the final form.
2025–2026 · ACTIVE · IN DEVELOPMENT
